Interactive Explorations

Understanding LeWM's
Representation Space

A collection of interactive visualizations that make tangible the mechanisms behind LeWorldModel — from patch tokenization to the SIGReg regularizer that prevents collapse.

Based on Maes, Le Lidec, Scieur, LeCun & Balestriero (2026)

LeWorldModel learns a compact representation space from raw video frames by predicting future states in embedding space rather than reconstructing pixels. The key insight is that only two loss terms are needed for stable end-to-end training: a prediction loss and the SIGReg regularizer.

Each visualization below isolates one concept from the architecture, making it explorable through direct interaction. Drag sliders, hover over elements, and step through animations to build intuition for how each piece works.
