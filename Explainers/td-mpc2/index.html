Interactive Explorations

TD-MPC2: Scalable, Robust
World Models

A collection of interactive visualizations that make tangible the key mechanisms behind TD-MPC2 — from MPPI trajectory planning to SimNorm's simplex projections to the scaling laws that emerge at 317M parameters.

Based on Hansen, Su & Wang (ICLR 2024, Spotlight)

TD-MPC2 learns an implicit, decoder-free world model that plans in latent space using trajectory optimization. A single agent with a single set of hyperparameters achieves state-of-the-art results across 104 continuous control tasks spanning 4 domains and 12 embodiments.

Each visualization below isolates one core idea from the architecture, making it explorable through direct interaction. Drag sliders, click data points, and step through simulations to build intuition for how each mechanism works.
