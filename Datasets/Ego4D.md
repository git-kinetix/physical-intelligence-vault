# Ego4D

## Description
Ego4D is the world's largest egocentric (first-person) video dataset, containing 3,600 hours of densely narrated daily life video captured by 926 unique camera wearers across 74 worldwide locations in 9 countries. It provides a benchmark suite with five tasks: episodic memory, hand-object interaction, audio-visual diarization, social interaction, and forecasting.

## Format
Egocentric video with dense temporal annotations, narrations, and benchmark-specific labels. Multiple data formats including raw video, extracted features, and annotations.

## Size
3,600 hours of video, >5 TB total. Subsets available for specific benchmarks.
