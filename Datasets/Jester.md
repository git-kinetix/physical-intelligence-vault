# Jester

## Description
The Jester Dataset is the largest dataset of video clips showing human hand gestures, created by TwentyBN (now Qualcomm AI Research). It contains short 3-second clips of crowd-sourced workers performing predefined hand gestures in front of a webcam, totaling over 5 million frames. It is primarily used for hand gesture recognition and temporal reasoning in video models.

## Format
Directories of JPEG frames (100px height, variable width), each directory corresponding to one video clip. Distributed as split TGZ archives.

## Size
148,092 video clips across 27 gesture classes. Approximately 22.8 GB compressed, ~54 GB uncompressed.
