# COIN

## Description
COIN (COmprehensive INstructional video dataset) is a large-scale dataset for instructional video analysis, covering 180 tasks across 12 everyday-life domains such as cooking, vehicle maintenance, gadget repair, and personal care. All videos are annotated with step-level descriptions and temporal boundaries, enabling research on procedure understanding, step localization, and task recognition.

## Format
YouTube video URLs with temporal annotations in JSON format. Annotations include hierarchical labels (domain, task, step) with start/end timestamps for each step.

## Size
11,827 videos covering 180 tasks across 12 domains, with 46,354 annotated step segments.

## License
Unknown. Available for research use.

## How to Download
Official website: https://coin-dataset.github.io/. Annotations available on GitHub: https://github.com/coin-dataset/annotations. Videos are sourced from YouTube and must be downloaded separately.
