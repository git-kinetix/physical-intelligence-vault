# HoloAssist

## Description
HoloAssist is a large-scale egocentric human interaction dataset from Microsoft Research for building interactive AI assistants in the real world. It spans 169 hours captured by 222 participants forming 350 instructor-performer pairs across 20 object-centric manipulation tasks, with seven raw sensor modalities: RGB, depth, head pose, 3D hand pose, eye gaze, audio, and IMU.

## Format
Multimodal egocentric data with RGB video, depth, head pose, 3D hand pose, eye gaze, audio, and IMU from HoloLens 2.

## Size
169 hours, 350 instructor-performer pairs, 222 participants, 20 tasks. ~1+ TB total.
