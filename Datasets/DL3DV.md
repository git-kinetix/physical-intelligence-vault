# DL3DV

## Description
DL3DV-10K is a large-scale scene dataset for deep learning-based 3D vision, containing over 10,000 high-quality videos captured across 65 types of point-of-interest locations. It provides multi-resolution video frames with estimated camera poses, enabling research on novel view synthesis, 3D reconstruction, and scene understanding at unprecedented scale and diversity.

## Format
Video frames at multiple resolutions (480p to 4K) with corresponding camera pose files. Organized by scene with COLMAP-estimated poses.

## Size
51.2 million frames from 10,510 videos. Storage varies by resolution: ~730 GB (480p), ~2.8 TB (960p), ~11 TB (2K), ~44 TB (4K).
