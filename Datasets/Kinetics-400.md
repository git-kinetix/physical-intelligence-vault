# Kinetics-400

## Description
Kinetics-400 is a large-scale video dataset of human actions created by DeepMind. It contains approximately 300,000 video clips covering 400 human action classes, with each class having at least 400 clips. Each clip is roughly 10 seconds long and is drawn from a unique YouTube video. It has become a standard benchmark for video understanding and action recognition.

## Format
Video clips (MP4) approximately 10 seconds long, sourced from YouTube. Provided as URL lists with temporal boundaries; videos must be downloaded from YouTube.

## Size
Approximately 300,000 video clips across 400 action classes. Raw video data is several hundred GB when downloaded.
