# RoboCasa

## Description
RoboCasa is a large-scale simulation framework for training generally capable robots to perform everyday household tasks, featuring realistic and diverse human-centered kitchen environments. It provides 365 tasks across 2,500+ kitchen scenes with over 2,200 hours of robot demonstration data (600+ hours human-collected, 1,600+ hours synthetically generated).

## Format
Simulated trajectory data with RGB images, depth, proprioceptive states, and actions in MuJoCo-based environments. Compatible with robomimic and robosuite frameworks.

## Size
365 tasks, 2,500+ scenes, 2,200+ hours of demonstration data.
