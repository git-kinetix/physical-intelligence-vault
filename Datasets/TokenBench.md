# TokenBench

## Description
TokenBench is a comprehensive video tokenizer evaluation benchmark curated by NVIDIA to standardize the evaluation of video tokenizers (specifically Cosmos-Tokenizer). It covers a wide variety of Physical AI-relevant domains including robotic manipulation, driving, egocentric video, and web videos, using high-resolution, long-duration videos to stress-test tokenizer reconstruction quality.

## Format
Video clips sourced from existing datasets (BDD100K, EgoExo-4D, BridgeData V2, Panda-70M) spanning multiple domains and resolutions.

## Size
Curated benchmark spanning multiple video categories (fish-eye, robotics, driving, human activities, spatial navigation). Exact total size depends on which source datasets are included.

## License
Unknown. Composed of clips from existing datasets with their own licenses.

## How to Download
Official GitHub: https://github.com/NVlabs/TokenBench. Part of the NVIDIA Cosmos tokenizer evaluation pipeline.
