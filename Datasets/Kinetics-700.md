# Kinetics-700

## Description
Kinetics-700 is an extended version of the Kinetics dataset family, expanding coverage to 700 human action classes with approximately 650,000 video clips. Each class contains at least 600 clips of roughly 10 seconds each, sourced from YouTube. It offers broader action vocabulary than Kinetics-400/600 for training and evaluating video understanding models.

## Format
Video clips (MP4) approximately 10 seconds long, sourced from YouTube. Provided as URL lists with temporal boundaries.

## Size
Approximately 650,000 video clips across 700 action classes. Several hundred GB when downloaded.
