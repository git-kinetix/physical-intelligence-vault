# Places205

## Description
Places205 is a scene recognition dataset developed at MIT CSAIL, containing 2.5 million images across 205 scene categories. It is designed to train and evaluate models on holistic scene understanding, complementing object-centric datasets like ImageNet. The categories span indoor and outdoor environments such as kitchens, bedrooms, forests, and stadiums.

## Format
JPEG images at variable resolution. A resized version at 256x256 pixels is commonly used.

## Size
2,448,873 images across 205 scene categories. Approximately 132 GB for the resized (256x256) version; ~1.8 TB for full-resolution images.
