# DreamDojo-HV

## Description
DreamDojo-HV (Human Videos) is the largest and most diverse egocentric video corpus for world model pretraining, containing over 44,711 hours of crowdsourced egocentric human video spanning 6,015 unique tasks and 1,135,000 trajectories. It features loco-manipulation skills across diverse environments including household, industrial, retail, educational, and administrative settings -- 15x longer, 96x more skills, and 2,000x more scenes than prior datasets.

## Format
Egocentric video sequences with latent action annotations for world model pretraining. Resolution 640x480.

## Size
44,711 hours of video, 1,135,000 trajectories, 6,015 tasks.
