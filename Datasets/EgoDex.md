# EgoDex

## Description
EgoDex is a large-scale egocentric dexterous manipulation dataset from Apple, containing 829 hours of 1080p, 30 Hz egocentric video with 338,000 episodes across 194 tasks, totaling 90 million frames. It includes paired 3D pose annotations for head, upper body, and hands, along with natural language annotations for learning dexterous manipulation from human demonstrations.

## Format
Egocentric video (1080p, 30 Hz) with 3D pose annotations (head, upper body, hands) and natural language descriptions.

## Size
2.0 TB on disk; 829 hours, 338,000 episodes, 90 million frames, 194 tasks.
