# TartanDrive

## Description
TartanDrive is the largest real-world, multimodal off-road driving dataset, collected by CMU's AirLab on a modified Yamaha Viking ATV. It contains approximately 200,000 off-road driving interactions with seven unique sensing modalities across diverse terrains, designed for learning dynamics models and autonomous off-road navigation.

## Format
Multimodal trajectory data including stereo RGB cameras, thermal camera, 2D LiDAR, 3D LiDAR (v2.0), GPS, IMU, and proprioceptive data (steering, throttle, velocity).

## Size
~200,000 interactions; TartanDrive 2.0 adds 7 hours of data at speeds up to 15 m/s.
