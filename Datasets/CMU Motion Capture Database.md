# CMU Motion Capture Database

## Description
The CMU Graphics Lab Motion Capture Database is a free, large-scale collection of human motion capture recordings covering a wide range of activities including walking, running, dancing, sports, and everyday actions. Captured by over 140 subjects, it has been one of the most widely-used motion capture resources for research in animation, biomechanics, and embodied AI.

## Format
Motion capture data in Acclaim (AMC/ASF) format. Community-converted versions available in BVH and FBX formats.

## Size
2,605 trials across 6 categories performed by 144 subjects. Multiple format conversions available.

## License
Free for research use. May be included in commercially-sold products, but the data itself may not be resold directly, even in converted form.
