# MVBench

## Description
MVBench is a comprehensive multi-modal video understanding benchmark published at CVPR 2024 that covers 20 challenging temporal video tasks organized across 8 categories. It is specifically designed so that tasks cannot be solved with a single frame, ensuring genuine temporal understanding evaluation with 200 multiple-choice QA pairs per task (4,000 total questions).

## Format
Video multiple-choice QA benchmark with 20 temporal tasks. Questions drawn from 34 diverse source datasets across 6 categories.

## Size
4,000 QA pairs (200 per task x 20 tasks), sourced from 34 datasets.
