# Ego-Exo4D

## Description
Ego-Exo4D is a large-scale multimodal dataset from Meta FAIR and 15 university partners that pairs egocentric and exocentric video perspectives of the same activities. The V2 release contains 1,286 hours of video (221 ego-hours) across 5,035 takes, enabling research on cross-view learning, video understanding, and multimodal perception.

## Format
Paired ego-exo video with temporal alignment, 3D body pose, gaze, audio, and activity annotations. Captured using Project Aria glasses (ego) and multiple synchronized cameras (exo).

## Size
~14 TiB recommended download; 1,286 hours of video, 5,035 takes (V2).
