# MVP

## Description
MVP (Multi-View Physical Prediction) is a video benchmark requiring physical world understanding, introduced alongside V-JEPA 2 by Meta AI. It evaluates models on their ability to predict and reason about physical phenomena from video, testing temporal and physical reasoning capabilities that go beyond single-frame understanding.

## Format
Video-based evaluation benchmark with multiple-choice questions and prediction tasks focused on physical reasoning.

## Size
Part of Meta's new physical reasoning benchmark suite for V-JEPA 2 evaluation.
