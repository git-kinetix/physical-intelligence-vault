# Scand

## Description
SCAND (Socially CompliAnt Navigation Dataset) is a large-scale dataset of human-teleoperated robot navigation demonstrations in social environments around the University of Texas at Austin campus. It contains 138 trajectories spanning 25 miles and 8.7 hours, with labeled tags for naturally occurring social interactions across diverse pedestrian scenarios.

## Format
ROS bags with multimodal data streams: 3D LiDAR, joystick commands, odometry, RGB images, and IMU. CSV exports provided for core modalities.

## Size
138 trajectories, 25 miles, 8.7 hours of teleoperated driving.
